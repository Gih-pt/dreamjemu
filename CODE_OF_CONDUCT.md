# Code of Conduct

This is a small hobby project. The expectations are simple:

- Be respectful. Disagree about code, architecture, and priorities as much as you like — that's normal and healthy — but keep it about the work, not the person.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language, in issues, PRs, or any other project space.
- Assume good faith, including toward AI-assisted contributions, which are explicitly welcome here (see `CONTRIBUTING.md` and `docs/AI_CONTRIBUTIONS.md`).
- Keep discussion focused on the project. This isn't the place for unrelated debates.

Maintainers may close issues/PRs or remove comments that violate this, without extensive explanation, to keep a small project's maintenance overhead manageable.
